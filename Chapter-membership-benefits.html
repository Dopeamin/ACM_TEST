Chapter membership benefits

11 Sep 2018

Joining INSAT ACM Student Chapter will provide you with many opportunities, as well as continuous support during the chapter's activities.
Participating in the chapter's activities are not exclusive to members. Any student is welcome to attend our events!

Joining the chapter

Joining the chapter conveys a profound interest and dedication to the computing discipline. The next step is volunteering in a variety of roles and capacities that can benefit the chapter and advance career opportunities as well. Members who get involved with ACM are creating networking and learning opportunities that advance both organizational and personal development! As a chapter member, participation in the chapter's activities will be heavily discounted, or free of charge!

INSAT Students

Only chapter members are financially supported if qualified to national and international stages of competitions, are eligible to elect, and be elected to chapter officers at the end of the academic year.
